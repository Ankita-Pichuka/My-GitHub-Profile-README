## 🌟 My Story

> *"Numbers have an important story to tell. They rely on you to give them a clear and convincing voice."* — Stephen Few

I started as a **Computer Science student** at Andhra University, curious about why data systems worked the way they did. That curiosity turned into a career — 3+ years building analytics pipelines, automating workflows with NLP, and crafting dashboards that actually get used.

Now I'm a **Graduate student in Analytics at Northeastern University (GPA: 3.83/4)**, pushing deeper into machine learning, AI systems, and predictive modeling — because I believe the best data stories are still unwritten.

When I'm not wrangling datasets, you'll find me editing videos 🎬 or experimenting in the kitchen 🍳
